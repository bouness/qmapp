📖 How to Use QMA

Please keep the developer, his father and teacher in your duʿāʾ. This app was inspired by the way he was taught as a child and developed to support others in learning and memorizing the Qur’an.

Quran Memorization Assistant (QMA): Select a Surah, set your verse range and repetition count, then press Start Playback.

Audio: Streamed from EveryAyah.com and cached for offline use after first play.

Text & translations: Loaded from local JSON files (assets/data/quran.json, assets/data/editions/*.json).

Install: Tap Install as App to add QMA to your home screen.
